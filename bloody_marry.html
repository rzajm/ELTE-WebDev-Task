Bloody Mary

Kcal: 123 Fat: 0g
Saturates: 0g Carbs: 7g
Sugars: 7g Fibre: 0g
Protein: 1g Salt: 0.6g
About

The Bloody Mary is a classic cocktail known for its bold, savory flavor and potential hangover-curing properties. It's made with vodka, tomato juice, and various spices and flavorings including Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. The cocktail is traditionally served over ice in a highball glass, often garnished with celery, olives, or lemon wedges. The origins of the Bloody Mary are unclear, but it has been a popular brunch beverage since the early 20th century.

Ingredients
  • 50ml vodka
  • 100ml tomato juice
  • 15ml lemon juice
  • 2 dashes Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dashes hot sauce
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • ice
  • celery stick, to garnish
What to do?
  • Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour the vodka, tomato juice, and lemon juice into the glass.
  • Add the Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Stir gently to combine.
  • Garnish with a celery stick. Serve immediately.
