Pina Colada

Kcal: 245 Fat: 1.5g
Saturates: 1g Carbs: 28g
Sugars: 24g Fibre: 0g
Protein: 1g Salt: 0.03g
About

The Pina Colada is a tropical cocktail that is sweet and creamy, made with rum, coconut cream, and pineapple juice. It is typically blended or shaken with ice and served in a tall glass, often garnished with a pineapple slice or a maraschino cherry. The Pina Colada is the national drink of Puerto Rico, where it was created in the 1950s. Its name means "strained pineapple" in Spanish, referring to the freshly pressed and strained pineapple juice used in the drink.

Ingredients
  • 50ml white rum
  • 25ml coconut cream
  • 25ml heavy cream
  • 200ml fresh pineapple juice
  • 100g crushed ice
  • pineapple slice and maraschino cherry, to garnish
What to do?
  • Put the rum, coconut cream, heavy cream, pineapple juice, and crushed ice into a blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Pour into a chilled glass.
  • Garnish with a pineapple slice and a maraschino cherry. Serve immediately.
