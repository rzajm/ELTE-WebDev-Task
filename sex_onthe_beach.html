Sex on the Beach

Kcal: 204 Fat: 0g
Saturates: 0g Carbs: 28g
Sugars: 28g Fibre: 0g
Protein: 0g Salt: 0g
About

The Sex on the Beach cocktail is a fun and fruity drink that is perfect for summer. It's made with vodka, peach schnapps, cranberry juice, and orange juice, served over ice in a highball glass. The vibrant colors and sweet, refreshing taste make it a favorite at parties and beach gatherings. Its origins are a bit murky, but it's been a popular cocktail since the 1980s, known for its playful name and delicious flavor.

Ingredients
  • 40ml vodka
  • 20ml peach schnapps
  • 50ml cranberry juice
  • 50ml orange juice
  • ice
  • orange slice or cherry, to garnish
What to do?
  • Fill a highball glass with ice cubes.
  • Pour the vodka and peach schnapps over the ice.
  • Add the cranberry juice and orange juice.
  • Garnish with an orange slice or a cherry. Serve immediately.
